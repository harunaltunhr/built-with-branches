If you are unemployed, looking for a new challenge or can’t find the skills you need for your company the European Job Days could be for you. Onsite or online, these Europe-wide recruitment fairs match jobseekers with employers in other European countries. Participants also get practical advice about living and working abroad.
Hundreds of Job Days take place each year in 32 countries. Employers get access to thousands of CVs for free whilst jobseekers get to apply for jobs across Europe in a variety of sectors – from medicine to tourism. Whether you are an experienced radiologist or a young person looking for an adventure in another country, the Job Days could have something to offer you.
You don’t have to be physically present at an event to get the benefits. Thanks to online chat tools and livestreaming you can ‘attend’ a Job Day from the comfort of your own home or office and even interview online. European Job Days are run by EURES, a network of European Employment Services, and the European Commission.
